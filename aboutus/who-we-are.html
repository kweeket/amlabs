Who we are

AmTest Inc. is a world class laboratory which has been in business since 1977. We are a woman-owned, small business providing service to all parts of Washington, Oregon, and beyond. Among our accomplishments as a company, we are proud to say that we have been accredited by the Washington State Department of Ecology for drinking water, waste water, and solid matrix.
